About Msaratech

Creating The Next Technology Leaders Since 2018

Msaratech is an innovative educational platform designed to empower students in medical schools with essential digital skills—particularly in artificial intelligence, data analytics, and modern automation tools. By bridging the gap between modern technology and traditional oriented medical studies in Egypt, Msaratech will be at the forefront of the digital medical transformation in Egypt, starting with the roots of the field, which are medical students.
We focus on providing a unique learning experience for students that combines academic learning in the various technical branches required for medical practice with hands-on experience on enhanced projects specifically designed for students, giving them the experience they need for the job market in that field. In addition to developing and implementing Ai Teaching Assistant to enhance the educational Experiences. By bridging the gap between technology and traditional fields such as medicine, law, and business, Msaratech offers a hybrid learning experience that combines self-paced recorded courses with live, hands-on sessions tailored to real-world professional challenges.
Msaratech's flexible business model—ranging from individual course purchases to corporate training solutions—positions it as a go-to platform for non-tech professionals seeking career-relevant tech education in the MENA region and beyond.
